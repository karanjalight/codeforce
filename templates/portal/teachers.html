Teachers

We pride ourselves on having a team of passionate and experienced educators who are dedicated to nurturing the academic and personal growth of our students. Each teacher brings unique expertise and commitment to ensuring that every student reaches their full potential. Our teachers not only focus on academic excellence but also foster creativity, critical thinking, and a love for lifelong learning.

Mrs. Wanjiru Mwangi

Head of English Department

Mrs. Mwangi is a seasoned English teacher with over 15 years of experience in guiding students through literature and language. Known for her engaging teaching methods and love for storytelling, she helps students excel in both creative and academic writing.

Mr. Kevin Otieno

Mathematics Teacher

Mr. Otieno is a dedicated mathematics teacher who simplifies complex concepts for his students. With a passion for problem-solving, he has helped students improve their grades significantly and develop a genuine interest in mathematics.

Ms. Esther Njeri

Science Department

Her hands-on approach, using fun experiments and nature walks, helps students develop a love for science and curiosity about the world around them.
